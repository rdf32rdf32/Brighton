Privacy Policy

This is an unofficial Brighton supporters’ site. It does not require user accounts and does not ask for personal information.

The site uses your browser’s local storage only to remember choices such as quiz progress, your selected team, match prediction, fixture month, sound and notice preferences on your own device.

No advertising or analytics services are included. The matchday weather panel contacts Open-Meteo only when a forecast is close enough to be useful.
