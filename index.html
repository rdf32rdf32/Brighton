History, landmarks and legends

Albion Story

The club’s journey, the grounds that shaped it and people remembered across generations.

1901

Brighton & Hove Albion are founded.

1910

Southern League champions and Charity Shield winners.

1983

Albion reach the FA Cup final at Wembley.

1997

League status is secured at Hereford.

2011

The Amex opens at Falmer.

2017

Promotion to the Premier League.

2023

First qualification for European football.

Goldstone Ground

Albion’s home from 1902 until 1997.

Priestfield Stadium

Two seasons ground-sharing at Gillingham.

Withdean Stadium

The club returned to Brighton in 1999.
