Albion history

From the Goldstone to Europe

1901

Brighton & Hove Albion are founded.

1910

Southern League champions and Charity Shield winners.

1983

Albion reach the FA Cup final at Wembley.

1997

League status is secured at Hereford after the final Goldstone season.

1999

The club returns to Brighton at Withdean Stadium.

2011

The Amex opens at Falmer.

2017

Promotion to the Premier League.

2023

First qualification for European football.
