Detailed matchday guide

Getting to the Amex

Choose a travel method for practical guidance. Arrangements can change for each match, so always check the club’s latest update before setting off.

Travel by train

  • Falmer station is immediately beside the stadium.
  • Services normally run from Brighton and Lewes, but matchday queues can be lengthy.
  • Allow extra time before kick-off and after the final whistle.
  • Check live railway information before travelling.

Bus and coach

  • Local buses serve Falmer and the surrounding area.
  • Eligible match tickets may include travel within the club’s designated travel zone.
  • Pre-booked supporter coaches may operate from several Sussex pick-up points.
  • Confirm the route and ticket conditions for your match.

Park and ride

  • Park-and-ride sites and opening times can vary by fixture.
  • Do not drive directly to the stadium without pre-arranged parking.
  • Follow the club’s match-specific travel notice and local signs.
  • Leave time for the return queue after the match.

Walking and cycling

  • The stadium can be reached using signed routes around Falmer.
  • Cycle parking is available, but bring a suitable lock.
  • Use well-lit routes and allow for large pedestrian crowds.
  • Check weather conditions before setting off.

Accessible travel

  • Plan assistance with the relevant transport operator in advance.
  • Accessible parking must normally be booked before matchday.
  • Contact the club for current drop-off, wheelchair and mobility guidance.
  • Allow additional time when leaving the stadium.

Visiting supporters

  • Check the visiting-fan guide and your ticket for the correct entrance.
  • Use official travel information rather than relying on ordinary non-matchday routes.
  • Parking near the stadium is extremely limited.
  • Follow steward directions when leaving the away section.

Albion glossary

Useful names and phrases heard around the club and the Amex.

Albion

The traditional short name for Brighton & Hove Albion.

Seagulls

The club nickname and the reply in the “Seagulls!” chant.

The Amex

The American Express Stadium at Falmer, Albion’s home since 2011.

Sussex by the Sea

The county song adopted as Albion’s anthem.

North Stand

The home end behind the goal, known for leading the atmosphere.

M23 derby

A common modern label for Albion’s rivalry with Crystal Palace.
