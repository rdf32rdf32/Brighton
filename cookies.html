Cookies and local storage

Albion Fan Hub does not use advertising or analytics cookies. It uses your browser’s local storage to remember quiz progress and best score, your selected matchday squad, saved prediction, fixture month, sound preference, welcome guide and acknowledgement of the storage notice.

This information remains on your device and is not sent to the site owner. Use Reset saved choices in the main page footer, or clear this site’s stored data in your browser settings, to remove it.

The weather panel requests public forecast information from Open-Meteo when a fixture is within the forecast window. The anthem is an audio file hosted with this website.
